A powerful declaration of faith and resilience, "Glory to God" sees Ry embracing his underdog mentality and fighter spirit. With infectious energy and authentic delivery, he weaves tales of struggle, ambition, and unwavering spiritual conviction throughout the track.

The production hits hard with sharp beats and booming 808s, matching Ry's confident flow as he navigates themes of redemption, gratitude, and staying true to himself. From day one with nothing but determination, "Glory to God" is an anthem for anyone grinding toward their dreams while keeping faith at the center.

With the hook "Glory to God, there's nothin else I wanna say," Ry centers the message on what truly matters – faith, family, and staying authentic in a game that constantly tries to change you.

A must-listen for fans of conscious hip-hop with substance and soul.
